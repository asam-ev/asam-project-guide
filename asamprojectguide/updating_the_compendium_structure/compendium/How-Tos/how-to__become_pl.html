How-To: Become Project Lead

Target Role

PROJECT LEAD

Eligible

WG MEMBER

Requirements

  • Commitment to project declared

  • Candidacy submitted

Motivation

To lead the direction of activities of a project.

When

Proposal phase

Summary

The project responsible and main content coordinator of a project.

Tasks

  • Coordinating project activities

  • Prioritizing project activities

  • Synchronization with Working Group Leads
To become the Project Lead of a project, you must first submit your candidacy in the Proposal phase.
