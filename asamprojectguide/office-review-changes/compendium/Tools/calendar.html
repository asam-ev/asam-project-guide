OpenX Calendar

Click the tool’s icon on the right to access the online tool directly.

ASAM has a shared calendar for all OpenX projects: the OpenX calendar (openx@asam.net). Members can subscribe to it and invite it to automatically keep track of all meetings related to any OpenX project.

It is mandatory for all OpenX projects to invite the OpenX calendar!
