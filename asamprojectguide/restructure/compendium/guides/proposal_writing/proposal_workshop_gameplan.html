Proposal workshop gameplan

PROJECT LEAD

When organizing a proposal workshop, a few things are needed as prerequisites.

Roles in the proposal process

Proposal submitter

The person who is responsible to drive the proposal.

Proposal writing team

A small group of persons writing the project proposal.

Proposal workshop member

An attendee of the proposal workshop.

ASAM office

The employees of ASAM.

Prerequisites

  1. Written proposal

  2. Proposal submitter is declared

  3. At least three companies committing to the project

Timeline to an approved project proposal

Hosting a proposal workshop

Face-to-face workshop

Duration

1 - 2 days

Location

ASAM Event Location

When organizing a face-to-face proposal workshop, a meeting room needs to be reserved depending on the amount of people. The following options are available at the ASAM office:

Online workshop

Duration

2 - 4 hours

Location

Microsoft Teams

Due to the fact that online meetings are more straining than face-to-face meetings, experience shows that proposal workshops need to be streamlined to reduce the duration to a maximum of 4 hours.
